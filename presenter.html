Keyboard & Touch Controls

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • → Space ↓ - Next slide
  • ← ↑ - Previous slide
  • Home - First slide
  • End - Last slide
  • O - Toggle overview mode
  • A - Toggle animations
  • F - Toggle fullscreen
  • P - Toggle progress bar
  • C - Toggle slide counter
  • T - Toggle timer
  • % - Toggle percentage
  • H - Hide/show all controls
  • R - Reset timer
  • ? - Show/hide this help
  • Esc - Exit presentation / Close modal

Touch/Mouse Controls:

  • Tap left side of screen - Previous slide
  • Tap right side of screen - Next slide
  • Tap center - No action (safe zone)
